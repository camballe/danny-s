Kenyan Pilau

Ingredients

2 onions
3 blended tomatoes
Grated ginger and garlic
Pre boiled beef
Black pepper
Beef masala
Garam masala
Soy sauce
Cumin
Pilau masala
Salt
Rice

Cooking Instructions

Put one capful of cooking oil then put the two chopped onions and cook till the onions turn golden brown


Blend the three tomatoes till pasty


The preferred spices are as follows :
Garam masala
Beef masala
Garlic powder
Black pepper


Put the blended tomatoes in cooking pot for 5-7 minutes


Measure equal amounts of the previously mentioned spices in the cooking pot


Put the spices in the cooking pot and stir


Put the grated ginger and garlic in the cooking pot and stir


Put the pre-boiled beef in the cooking pot and stir


Cover the food with a lid and let it cook for 10-15 minutes


Measure one and a half cups of rice


Separate chaff from rice


Wash and rinse rice twice


Measure three cups of water and add to the cooking pot and wait for 5-10 minutes


Use pilau masala and salt


Add prefered amount of your choice of pilau masala and salt and stir


Put rice after the water has boiled and reduce heat and wait for 10 - 20 minutes


Serve while hot
