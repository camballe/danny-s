Hashbrowns

Ingredients

2 medium potatoes,peeled
1/2 medium onion,freshly chopped
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1 egg
1 cup oil for frying
Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

Peel and rinse the shredded potatoes.Drain and squeeze dry.Place shreds in a bowl and mix in onion,flour and egg until eventually distributed.


Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.When oil is hot place potatoes into the pan.


Cook until nicely browned.Then flip over and brown the other side.


Remove from pan and drain on paper towel.Season with salt and pepper.serve immediately.
