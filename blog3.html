Butternut Soup

Ingredients

Butternut
Potatoes
Cooking oil
Water
Onions
Cumin
Black pepper

Cooking Instructions

Peel the potatoes and the butternut and cut them in small pieces.


Fry the onions and add butternut and potatoes. Then add black pepper.


Add water and cover to cook.


When well cooked, put it into the blender to blend till there's no pieces of the butternut or potatoes. Blend them to your consistency as you add little warm water.


Serve your soup in a bowl and enjoy with bread.
